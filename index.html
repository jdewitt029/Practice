Practice (2019)

Illustrator
1722x2525 px

This is a piece I madeusing Illustrator to create an animated version of my friend. I asked many of my friends to send headshots of themselves so I could practice the art of tranferring a real photo to a vector image. The process consists of first drawing all the lines from the original process, coloring the piece, then adding all the highlights/shading. I am still new to art in general so having to add different value and shade really helped me out in my art overall.
